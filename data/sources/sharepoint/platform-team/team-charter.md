# Platform Team Charter

## Mission
The Platform Team is responsible for building and maintaining the following services:
- auth-service
- api-gateway
- config-service
- user-service

## Responsibilities
- Service reliability and uptime (99.9% SLA)
- Feature development and maintenance
- Security patching and dependency updates
- On-call rotation for owned services
