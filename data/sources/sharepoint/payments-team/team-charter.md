# Payments Team Charter

## Mission
The Payments Team is responsible for building and maintaining the following services:
- payment-processor
- billing-service
- invoice-service
- stripe-gateway

## Responsibilities
- Service reliability and uptime (99.9% SLA)
- Feature development and maintenance
- Security patching and dependency updates
- On-call rotation for owned services
