# Infrastructure Team Charter

## Mission
The Infrastructure Team is responsible for building and maintaining the following services:
- deploy-service
- monitoring-stack
- logging-service
- cdn-manager

## Responsibilities
- Service reliability and uptime (99.9% SLA)
- Feature development and maintenance
- Security patching and dependency updates
- On-call rotation for owned services
